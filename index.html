    How Hire30A keeps locals connected

    Three simple tracks bring the marketplace to life. Each is open now so you can start sharing the story before launch.

    1. Sync the job feed

    Our aggregator checks coastal keywords, hospitality categories, and local neighborhoods. Admins can refresh at any time to capture the latest postings and remove duplicates.

    • LinkedIn, Indeed, ZipRecruiter, Google Jobs through JSearch API
    • SoWal forums, regional chambers, partner ATS exports
    • Filterable by neighborhood & service type
    View aggregated roles

    2. Elevate local talent

    Service pros build a shareable profile highlighting guest-obsessed experience, certifications, and schedule preferences. Export a digital calling card or share with employers instantly.

    • Drag-and-drop highlights & skill tags
    • Portfolio links, availability, and introduction video slots
    • One-click export to share with coastal employers
    Create a talent profile

    3. Showcase hiring brands

    Restaurants, resorts, venues, and vacation rental managers craft a culture-rich profile with perks, brand colors, and shift needs so candidates can fall in love with your story.

    • Visual storytelling with photo & social embeds
    • Flexible shifts, pay transparency, and team values
    • Signal interest in future paid sponsorships
